Get Started

  • Instant Meeting Transcripts

    Keep track of everything that's said with automated, real-time transcription. After the meeting, you can review the transcript to recall important details, decisions, and action items. Plus, you can save, search, and share the transcripts for future reference.

  • Real-Time Name Alerts

    Never miss when your name is mentioned. CallSidekick sends an alert and displays a transcript of what was last said in the video call when your name is spoken. It's the perfect feature for large meetings or when you're multitasking.

  • Nothing to Install

    CallSidekick runs completely in your web browser. There's no plugins or programs to install!

  • Speaker Identification

    Know who said what. We distinguish between individual speakers, making the transcript easier to read and understand.

  • Custom Alerts

    Customize your alert preferences to include other keywords besides your name. Stay informed about the topics you care about, even when you're not actively participating in the conversation.

  • Private and Secure

    Your transcripts remain private. Transcripts will never be used for markerting or read by anyone other than you.
